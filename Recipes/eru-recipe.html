Eru-recipe

Description

The eru dish is a cultural dish of the manyu people of southwestern cameroon
and has become a worldwide dish thanks to its irrestible flavor.

Ingredients

  • eru leaves
  • palm oil
  • waterleafs
  • red pepper
  • seasoning maggi cubes and salt
  • beef and beef skin
  • smoked fish
  • crayfish
  • onion

Steps

  1. prepare your meat and beef skin and chop them to your desired sizes, wash them and put them in a clean pot alongside water,sliced onions,2 maggie cubes and a pinch of salt.set the pot on a lit stove and let it cook for 10mins.
  2. while the pot is cooking,wash your already sliced eru leaves and waterleaves to remove any sand on them, strain them with a sieve and set them in two seperate bowls to use later.
  3. check the pot on the stove and test the beef to make sure it is well cooked to your desired taste and then start by adding the waterleaves to the pot cooking on the stove with the beef,stir thepot with a wooden spoon and cover it to cook for a minute.
  4. open the pot and start adding the eru leaves into the mixture and stir with the wooden spoon while adding to homogenise the mix of the eru leaves with the with all the other ingredients and cover the lid of the pot and let it cook for 2minute intervals while stiring until the eru leaves are softin the mouth when you taste them.
  5. then add red oil into the mix,close the lid and let it cook for two minutes and then stir with a wooden spoon and then cover the lid and let it cook again for 5 minutes. open the lid and stir while tasting the food and making sure salt and maggie cubes taste as you desire then close the pot one last time and let it cook for 5 more minutes and if the eru is soft and taste just the way you want it, turn off the stove and your eru is ready.But if the eru is still strong when you taste it,repeat the above stepp several times till you get the desired soft eru.
